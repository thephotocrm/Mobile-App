When you grant permission, we may access:

**Photos and Camera**
- Access to your photo library to upload images to client galleries

- Calendar events are only created or modified with your explicit action

**Location**
- Location data to tag photos with venue information

- Location is only accessed when the App is in use, not in the background

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored on secure servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard cloud infrastructure providers that maintain appropriate physical, technical, and administrative safeguards.

### How We Protect Your Data

We implement multiple layers of security:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
- **Encryption at Rest:** Sensitive data is encrypted when stored on our servers
- **Secure Authentication:** Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- **Access Controls:** Only authorized personnel can access user data, and only when necessary for support or maintenance
- **Regular Security Audits:** We conduct periodic reviews of our security practices

### Local Data Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device for offline access and performance:
- Cached project and client information
- App preferences and settings
- Authentication tokens (stored in secure device storage)

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Portability

You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format
- Review and update your account information at any time through the App

### Correction

You can update or correct your personal information directly within the App's settings and profile sections.

### Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and associated data:
- Use the "Delete Account" option in the App's Settings screen
- Contact us at privacy@thephotocrm.com to request deletion
- Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
- Upon deletion, we will remove your personal data from our active systems within 30 days
- Some data may be retained in backups for up to 90 days before complete removal
- We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention)

### Opt-Out Options

You can:
- Disable push notifications through your device settings
- Revoke device permissions (camera, photos, contacts, calendar, location) through your device settings
- Note: Revoking certain permissions may limit App functionality

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

**Legal Basis for Processing**
We process your data based on:
- **Contract:** To provide the services you requested
- **Consent:** Where you have given explicit consent
- **Legitimate Interests:** To improve our services and ensure security

**Your GDPR Rights**
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction:** Request limited processing of your data
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

**Data Protection Authority**
You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we have not complied with applicable data protection laws.

**Contact Our Data Protection Team**
For GDPR-related inquiries: privacy@thephotocrm.com
